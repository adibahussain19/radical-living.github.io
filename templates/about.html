About Us

History

Formed in 2015, Radical Living grew out of a Christian co-housing community founded by Vonetta and Jason Storbakken. The community was organized as a church plant under the auspices of the Mennonite Church USA, a denomination oriented toward peace and social justice, non-violent resistance, and faith formation as key themes of Christian witness.

Mission

Radical Living is an asset-based community organization (501c3) that aims to build community around food, sustainable practices, and green spaces by supporting resident-led efforts to increase healthy food options, educate residents about their role in the food system, and reconnect residents to food, earth, and each other.
